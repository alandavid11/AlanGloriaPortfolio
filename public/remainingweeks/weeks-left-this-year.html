Quick answers

How many weeks are in a year?

A regular year has 365 days — that's 52 full weeks plus one extra day (leap years have two extra days). That single spare day is why the calendar shifts by one weekday every year.

How is this calculated?

We count the days from today through December 31 and divide by seven, rounding down to full weeks. No time zones tricks — it uses your device's local date.

How many weekends are left?

One per remaining week. If there are 24 full weeks left, you have about 24 Saturdays — it's a surprisingly small number when you see it written down.

This number hits different when it's your whole life.

RemainingWeeks shows your weeks — this year, this month, or your entire life — on your iPhone Home Screen, Lock Screen and Apple Watch, with a new square filling in every Monday.
