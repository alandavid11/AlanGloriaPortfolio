How do I set my life grid as wallpaper?

Open the app → Settings → Wallpaper to export an image, or use the included Shortcut. iOS doesn't let apps change your wallpaper automatically, so the app provides a Shortcuts action ("Generate RemainingWeeks wallpaper") that you can run — or automate to run daily — together with the system "Set Wallpaper" action.

Can a widget or watch complication show a specific countdown?

Yes. Long-press a widget (or edit a watch complication) and choose the countdown in its configuration. Leave it empty to show your life view.

My widget or complication looks empty on Apple Watch.

Open the iPhone app once so it can sync your data to the watch, then the complication will update. Watch complications refresh roughly once a day.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings inside the app and tap Restore purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple: on your device open Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions, or App Store → your profile → Subscriptions. The annual plan's free trial converts to a paid subscription unless cancelled at least 24 hours before it ends.

Does my data sync across devices?

If you're signed in to iCloud, your profile, countdowns and reflections sync privately through your iCloud account. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Is the life expectancy exact?

No — it's an estimate based on your country's average life expectancy, meant for reflection and perspective, not prediction. It is not medical advice.
