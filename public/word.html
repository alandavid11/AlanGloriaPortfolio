How it works

One word at a time — the RSVP method

Most people read around 200–250 words per minute. What slows you down isn't your brain — it's your eyes jumping between words and quietly re-reading lines you already finished.

RSVP (Rapid Serial Visual Presentation) removes both problems: words appear one by one in a fixed spot, with a highlighted focal letter your eyes lock onto. No eye movement, no back-skipping — just comprehension at higher speed. WoRD turns that technique into a daily workout: short sessions, gradual speed increases, and challenges that push your limit without losing understanding.
