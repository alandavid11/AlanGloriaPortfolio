Frequently asked

Why aren't outfit suggestions appearing?

Add a few garments to your trip first, then tap Generate outfits. Suggestions need an internet connection.

Do my photos leave my phone?

Only background-removed garment images are sent for AI labeling. Your Lookbook selfies stay on your device and private iCloud and are never uploaded.

Does my data sync between devices?

Yes — through your personal iCloud account. Make sure you're signed in to iCloud with the same Apple ID.

How do I manage my subscription?

Open Settings → Manage subscription in the app, or your Apple ID subscription settings.
