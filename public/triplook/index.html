Pack like a stylist.

Photograph your clothes once and Triplook builds the rest: an AI-tagged digital closet, weather-smart outfits for every day of your trip, packing lists that build themselves, and a private lookbook of what you actually wore.

Free · iPhone & iPad · English & Español

What it does

Everything between “where are we going?” and a packed suitcase.

AI garment scan

Snap a photo — the background disappears and every piece is tagged by category, color, warmth and formality.

Weather-smart outfits

One look per trip day, matched to the real forecast at your destination — sunny café mornings to cool evenings.

Look studio

A carousel per category, stacked like a mannequin. Tap to pick, shuffle for inspiration, save the look.

Packing lists that build themselves

Your list is derived from the outfits you planned — with photos, wear counts and check-off as you pack.

A real outfit calendar

Trips render as bands across their dates; office, gym and dinner plans get outfits too — not just travel.

Private lookbook

Keep photos of what you actually wore, grouped by trip. Selfies stay on your device and private iCloud.

Privacy as architecture: Triplook has no accounts and no server database. Your closet, plans and photos live in your private iCloud; only background-removed garment images are used for AI styling.
