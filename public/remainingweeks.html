RemainingWeeks is a quiet, minimalist memento mori calendar. It turns your life expectancy into a grid of weeks — so the time you have left stops being abstract.

 Download on the App Store

What it does

A calmer way to look at time

Life in weeks

Your whole life as a grid based on your country's life expectancy. Filter by life, this year, or this month — in days, weeks, months or years.

Custom countdowns

A trip, a goal, retirement. Track any future date with its own grid, widgets and wallpaper.

Widgets & lock screen

Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets that always match your active filter, refreshed daily.

Apple Watch

Complications showing your life grid or any countdown, in every watch-face family.

Wallpaper via Shortcuts

Export a wallpaper of your grid and let a Shortcut refresh it automatically each day.

Reflect & stay aware

Daily inspiration, milestone alerts, and a one-line weekly reflection journal.

Built with

Native, private, polished

SwiftUI SwiftData WidgetKit watchOS App Intents StoreKit 2 iCloud sync
